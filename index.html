The Opportunity

Tile Beyond the Catalog

Standard tile manufacturing is driven by mold economics — the same 300 forms repeated across millions of square meters. Ceramic 3D printing breaks this constraint: every tile can be different, every surface unique.

Parametric tile design tools let architects and interior designers define rules rather than shapes — generating field-to-field variation, acoustic tuning, or brand-specific surface texture across any installation scale.

Interior Wall Tile

Exterior Cladding

Sound-absorbing ceramic wall systems for office, auditorium, and restaurant environments with tunable geometry.

Perforated Screens

Decorative and functional ceramic privacy screens and room dividers with parametric aperture patterns.
