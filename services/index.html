Our Process

How We Work

1

Design Review

We evaluate your design for printability — wall thickness, unsupported spans, and material suitability for your target ceramic system.

2

Material Selection

Choose the right clay body, paste system, or slurry for your application. We advise on shrinkage, firing temperature, and post-process requirements.

3

Print & Green Body

Parts are printed and inspected before thermal processing. Green body quality determines final dimensional accuracy.

4

Firing & Finishing

Controlled debinding and sintering. Glazing and surface finishing to your specification.

5

Inspection & Delivery

Dimensional inspection, photography, and packaging. Shipped with full material and process documentation.
